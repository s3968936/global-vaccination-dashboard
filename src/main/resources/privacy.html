Your privacy and data protection

Privacy Policy

At the Global Health Dashboard, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may share whilst using this platform. This privacy policy outlines how we handle data on this educational project.

Information We Collect

This platform is an educational project developed for COSC2803 at RMIT University. We do not collect sensitive personal data unless voluntarily provided through forms (e.g., feedback submissions). Any information submitted is used solely for educational purposes and platform improvement.

How We Use Your Information

Any data collected through feedback forms is used to:

  • Improve the user experience and functionality of the platform
  • Understand user needs and platform effectiveness
  • Respond to enquiries and feedback

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties.

Data Security

We use standard security measures to safeguard information stored on our systems. As this is an educational project, we encourage users to avoid submitting sensitive personal information through any forms on this platform.

Your Consent

By using this platform, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you have any questions or concerns about how your information is handled, please be aware this is a student project and data handling follows RMIT University's guidelines for educational purposes.

Note: This is an educational project. For demonstration purposes only.
