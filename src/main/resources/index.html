Welcome to the Global Health Dashboard

Vaccination has been instrumental in eradicating diseases like smallpox and achieving global herd immunity for others, such as polio. This platform provides comprehensive, unbiased data from the World Health Organisation (WHO) to help diverse stakeholders—researchers, policymakers, healthcare professionals, and concerned citizens—make informed decisions about global immunisation efforts.

What you can explore:

  • Vaccination Coverage: Track immunisation rates across countries, regions, and economic groups
  • Disease Trends: Monitor infection rates and outbreaks of preventable diseases worldwide
  • Regional Comparisons: Analyse vaccination progress across different geographical areas
  • Economic Impact: Understand how economic status affects vaccination rates and health outcomes

Explore the data below to discover key insights, or use our navigation menu to dive deeper into specific areas of interest.
