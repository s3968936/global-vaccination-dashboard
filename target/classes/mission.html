Our Mission

The Global Health Dashboard is dedicated to promoting awareness and understanding of vaccination and infection trends through accessible, data-driven insights. Our mission is to address the critical social challenge of preventable infectious diseases by providing comprehensive, unbiased information that empowers diverse stakeholders.

How This Website Addresses the Social Challenge

Vaccination has been instrumental in eradicating diseases like smallpox and achieving global herd immunity for others, such as polio. However, understanding global immunisation efforts requires access to detailed, trustworthy data. Our platform bridges this gap by:

  • Presenting unbiased data: We provide comprehensive WHO data in an informative and respectful manner
  • Supporting diverse users: From healthcare professionals to concerned citizens, our platform caters to various levels of expertise
  • Enabling informed decisions: Through both high-level summaries and in-depth analysis tools, users can explore data that matters to them
  • Promoting transparency: Clear visualisations and accessible filters help users understand vaccination coverage, disease trends, and health outcomes

How to Use This Website

Navigate through our platform to discover insights tailored to your needs:

  • Homepage: View key statistics at a glance to understand global vaccination trends
  • Explore Data: Filter and analyse vaccination rates by country, region, and time period
  • Trending: Discover patterns in infection data across different economic groups
  • Insights: Access deep-dive analyses including vaccination improvements and infection rate comparisons
  • Feedback: Share your thoughts and help us improve the platform

Understanding Our Target Users

To ensure this platform meets the needs of real people, we've developed personas detailed profiles representing our target user groups. These personas guide our design decisions and help us create a user experience that serves diverse needs effectively.

Each persona represents a different stakeholder in global health, from healthcare professionals seeking data for policy recommendations to concerned parents researching vaccination information for their families. By understanding their goals, needs, and skills, we can ensure our platform provides relevant, accessible information for everyone.

Project Contributors

This project was developed as part of COSC2803 Java Programming Studio 1 at RMIT University. The implementation was divided into two parallel sub-task streams (A and B), each covering three complexity levels: Green (foundational), Orange (intermediate), and Red (advanced).

Sub-Task Breakdown

Sub-Task A

  • Level 1 (Green): Landing Page with data snapshot
  • Level 2 (Orange): Focused view of vaccination rates by country and region
  • Level 3 (Red): Identify countries with biggest improvement in vaccination rates

Sub-Task B

  • Level 1 (Green): Mission Statement and Personas
  • Level 2 (Orange): Focused view of infection data by economic status
  • Level 3 (Red): Identify countries with above average infection rates
